to cap the semester i took a Study Skills course, i made this application only to find out that I made something actually useful and will need it in my studies ever since.

i was scrolling through the internet when i came upon a reel about Japanese textbook with a red filter when placed on the page conceals the answers and when removed the answers show. That is the physics of light and how red filters absord red light and hence you nolonger see the red ink beneath it. But i was struck by the application of that principle in that context and furthermore how philosophically the answer (knowledge) is always on the page but only through certain lens can it be finally perceived.

i then thought to create an app that, just like camera filters emphasize certain colors and bring out certain vibes from one picture, the same is applicable to textbook text, how it can be consumed in more than just reading it but also through transforming it, manipulating it, mining it and applying certain study skills filters to it.

StudyGenius is a premium, AI-powered study assistant designed to transform raw study material into interactive, cognitively optimized learning experiences.

It uses Google's Gemini 2.5 Flash to analyze text or uploaded PDFs and applies specific "Cognitive Filters" to help you learn better, faster, and with more engagement.
